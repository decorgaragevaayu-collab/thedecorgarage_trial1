Frequently Asked Questions

Yes! We specialize in custom mirror and decor designs. Share your vision with us, and our team will work with you to create the perfect piece for your space.

What are your delivery options?

We offer delivery across India. Delivery time varies based on your location and product availability. We'll provide an estimated timeline when you place your order.

Absolutely! We can share detailed photos and videos of our products via WhatsApp. For custom orders, we provide design previews before production.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for manufacturing defects. Custom-made items are non-returnable unless there's a quality issue. Contact us for detailed terms.
